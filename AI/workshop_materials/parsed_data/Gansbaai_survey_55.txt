# What are the advantages of farming in this location?
Water is clean - no factories close by that contaminate water. Semi-remote so was less of a security risk but becoming much more developed and accessible.

# What are the disadvantages of farming in this location?
Labour force is further away - has to spend more on transport to get workers here. Distance to the airport. Trucks carrying abalone are a security risk so need escort.

# What are the biggest natural risks to your farming?
Algal blooms and red tide reduce water quality and can make abalone toxic. Wind damage recently. Ocean situation - pump house is close to ocean so high waves/ swell could damage. Water pH changes.
